The delivery driver will deliver cakes, balloons and additional retail items ordered by our guests either to homes or businesses as well as deliveries and errands for management. The deliveries will be made during the business day in a timely manner with a professional presentation and a signature from the recipient. The driver will need to carry brochures and have a good knowledge of TOH's products and services as he/she will be asked many questions while doing deliveries. The driver will be responsible for logging mileage, keeping the delivery vehicle clean and will work with management to keep it maintained and running properly. The driver will stay in touch with the bakery throughout the day by calling and being available to take calls at a safe time.
Salary - $20k-$44k
